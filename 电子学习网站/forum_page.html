The task & The scenerio

May i ask the difference between the tasks and the task scenerio? Would you briefly give examples for each of them?

Task scenario is a kind of story that can include different tasks for conducting a usability test.

For example, a task scenario can be Ali wants to use an online shopping website to purchase a laptop.

Based on this sample task scenario, we can define three different tasks:

• Open the website and search for a laptop

• Add the laptop into the shopping cart

 • Complete the purchase process
